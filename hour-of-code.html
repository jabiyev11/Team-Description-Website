Hour Of Code

We are thrilled to share that on November 1, 2023, Team Matrix consisting of Javid Jabiyev,
Emin Dabakhov, Muaataz Abdulhakeem Ismaeel, and Nazrin Baghirova organized
an exciting Hour of Code event at Secondary School No. 145. Our mission was to introduce
the fundamentals of programming to the 8A class, which was comprised of 21 students. Throughout
the event, we engaged in a coding adventure with the students, covering basic algorithms and
helping them explore the world of programming and technology using Code.org. We extend our
gratitude to the school administration for generously supporting us and making this event possible.
We are excited about the future of the young talented and inspiring next generation of coders.
